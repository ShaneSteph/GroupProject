Background Information

RUMAH ANAK YATIM SULTAN SALEHUDDIN ABDUL AZIZ SHAH (RAYSAS)

ADMINISTRATOR'S NAME: EN NALILUS HAFIZ BIN IBRAHIM

YEAR FOUNDED: 15 NOVEMBER 2000

The idea of establishing this orphanage was initiated by YAB Datuk Seri Modh Ali Rustam who is the Chief Minister of Malacca as one of the efforts to improve the standard of living of those who are less fortunate among orphans.

RAYSS Melaka was built on Lot 1159 Mukim Tangga Batu, Melaka with an area of ​​1.3 acres donated by Datuk H.M and Lot 816 land with an area of ​​0.4 acres which was donated by the Mahat bin Nekma family.

It was completed and started operating on 13 March 2002 with the recruitment of a total of 100 selected residents from among orphans throughout the state of Melaka.

Facilities provided
1.Comfortable and safe dormitory accommodation that accommodates 4 to 8 children. Has a locker for each student and also a clean and comfortable toilet.
2. Food and drinks are provided and scheduled five times a day. RAYSAS has a large and comfortable dining hall and even the food menu follows the guidelines provided by KPM.
3. Have learning in the hostel, i.e. tuition classes, halaqah classes, studying classes in the hostel on a scheduled basis. RAYSAS has sufficient teaching staff and even monitoring the level of student learning is implemented to improve the performance of each student.
4. Activities to fill each student's free time are always held to strengthen friendships such as programs with various agencies and NGOs, skill classes such as self-defense classes, cooking classes. RAYSAS also organizes student motivation and days with clients to meet guardians to discuss student development.
